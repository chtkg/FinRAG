问题：特斯拉如何提高其汽车的性价比，并且这一策略是否有效？
答案：Tesla enhances cost-effectiveness by collaborating with Panasonic through the Gigafactory 1, which specializes in battery production. This partnership allows Tesla to gain expertise and knowledge in electric cell manufacturing processes. By potentially producing its own batteries in the future, Tesla aims to reduce costs associated with external suppliers, making its vehicles more cost-effective.
