问题：若灵活用工业务的派遣员工数和人均服务费增速分别为20%和8%，那么根据提供的信息，19-21年的收入增速应该是多少？
答案：The estimated annual revenue growth rate for the period from year 19 to year 21 was as follows: 34.3% in year 19, 35.0% in year 20, and 29.6% in year 21. These figures were based on a 20% increase in dispatched workers in flexible industrial operations and an 8% annual rise in per capita service fees.
