问题：根据移动数据流量的变化趋势，云基础设施投资将如何变化，并影响哪些公司？
答案：The increasing demand for various technologies like cloud computing, big data, 5G, IoT, wireless charging, servers, NFC, electric vehicles, and inverters suggests that there will be a surge in mobile data traffic trends. This growth in mobile data traffic is expected to significantly influence investments in cloud infrastructure as more services shift online and require robust data processing capabilities. Companies heavily involved in these sectors, such as cloud service providers, telecommunications firms, and technology manufacturers, are likely to experience increased demand for their products and services due to this trend.
