问题：公司旗下餐饮品牌涉猎哪些细分市场，以及这些市场的预计复合增速如何？
答案：The company's restaurant portfolio targets the traditional cuisine market in Northwest China, which includes food from five provinces (Shaanxi, Gansu, Qinghai, Ningxia, and Xinjiang). The projected compound annual growth rate for this market is approximately 10%, with an expectation to reach 2854 billion yuan by 2024, starting from 1682 billion yuan in 2018.
