问题：氢燃料电池汽车的发展现状和未来展望是什么？
答案：Hydrogenics is a global leader in fuel cell stack technology, electrolysis water hydrogen production, and the operation of hydrogen stations. Snowman Corporation has developed 10 types of fuel cell systems based on Hydrogenics' technology. In 2019, they signed a cooperation agreement with the Liangjiang New Area of Chongqing to manufacture and develop PEMFC (Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cell) engines and their core components. These developments indicate ongoing advancements in hydrogen fuel cell vehicle technology and production capacity.
