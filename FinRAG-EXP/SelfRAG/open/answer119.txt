问题：随着渗透率的提升，未来我国辅助生殖服务市场规模预计会达到什么水平？
答案：The projected scale of China's future assisted reproductive services market, as its penetration rate increases, is expected to reach 496 billion yuan by 2023. This growth is estimated based on a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 14.5% from 2018 onwards. The market size was approximately 252 billion yuan in 2018, indicating significant potential for expansion as the penetration rate improves compared to the current 7% and the US's 30.2%.
