问题：长期来看，如何评估疫情对产业链和供应链的整体影响？这些影响会带来什么样的机遇或挑战？
答案：Evaluating the long-term impact of a pandemic on global supply chains and industries involves analyzing changes in trade policies, market disruptions, and recovery strategies. The opportunities may include enhanced digitalization and automation to improve resilience, while challenges could be increased costs, supply chain vulnerabilities, and potential shifts in consumer behavior towards local products.
