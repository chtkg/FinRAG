问题：随着算力的重要性逐步凸显和海量数据的增长，未来人工智能服务器市场可能会有怎样的变化和影响？
答案：The increasing significance of computing power and the rise in big data are likely to drive a surge in demand for AI servers. As seen from IDC's data, the market for AI servers reached $16.81 billion in 2018, accounting for over 60% of China's AI market. Moreover, the demand for AI training tasks has grown by more than 300,000 times since 2012, with a doubling rate every 3.43 months. IDC predicts that by 2020, the computational requirements for AI will increase by another 12 times, highlighting the growing importance of computing power in shaping the future landscape and dynamics of the AI server market.
