问题：影响氢燃料电池汽车发展的关键因素有哪些？
答案：The advancement of hydrogen fuel cell vehicle technology is shaped by several critical factors, including the development and production capabilities in areas such as fuel cell stack technology (as exemplified by Hydrogenics' world-leading expertise in fuel cell stacks, electrolysis water production, and hydrogen station operations). Collaborations with leading companies like Hydrogenics also play a significant role. Additionally, research and innovation are key, as seen in the development of advanced PEMFC systems by New Source Power, which includes the HYSTK-70 model with a rated power of 70kW and peak power reaching 85kW, showcasing an impressive volume power density of over 3.3 kW/L.
