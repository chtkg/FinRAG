问题：中国在全球燃料电池领域的研究地位如何？
答案：China's standing in the global fuel cell sector is notable, with companies like Hydrogenics and Snowman Corporation showcasing advanced capabilities. Hydrogenics leads in areas such as fuel cell stack technology, water electrolysis for hydrogen production, and the operation of hydrogen refueling stations. Meanwhile, Snowman Corporation has developed 10 types of fuel cell systems based on Hydrogenics' technology. In addition to these advancements, new sources of power have also made significant strides with their third-generation thin metal bipolar plate PEMFC stack, HYSTK-70, which boasts a rated power of 70kW and peak power up to 85kW, achieving an impressive volume power density of over 3.3 kW/L.
