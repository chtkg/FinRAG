问题：路博欣公司的主要业务特点是什么？
答案：The main business attributes of Roubi Xin company include its focus on Northwest Chinese cuisine through its flagship brand, Jiemiaojiujiu. This brand is the first chain restaurant in South China specializing in Northwest dishes and targets families with a menu featuring around 60-80 items including Shanxi noodles and other home-style dishes. The company updates its menu every quarter based on customer feedback, replacing approximately 20-30% of dishes annually.
