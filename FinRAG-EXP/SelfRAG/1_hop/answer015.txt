问题：中国现代农业发展面临的主要挑战是什么？
答案：The main challenges in modernizing agriculture in China include integrating traditional agricultural input manufacturing processes, leveraging big data and the Internet of Things (IoT) effectively, and establishing a comprehensive research-to-product-to-service industrial chain through platforms like Modern Agriculture Platform (MAP). These challenges are part of efforts to provide farmers with full-service solutions within the Sinograin agricultural system.
