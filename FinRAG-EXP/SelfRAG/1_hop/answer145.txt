问题：特斯拉在中国市场的主要挑战和机遇是什么？
答案：The main challenges Tesla faces in China's auto industry include the escalating conflict with Panasonic over car sales, battery production capacity, and battery pricing. Additionally, Tesla is encountering difficulties as it seeks to reduce its reliance on Panasonic for technology by integrating more deeply into the battery supply chain and diversifying its supplier base.
