问题：文章中提到的氢燃料电池汽车产业链中的主要公司有哪些？
答案：The primary companies involved in the hydrogen fuel cell vehicle supply chain, as mentioned in the article, are Hydrogenics and Snowman Corporation. Hydrogenics is recognized for its world-leading expertise in fuel cell stacks, water electrolysis for hydrogen production, and the operation of hydrogen refueling stations. Snowman Corporation has developed 10 types of fuel cell systems based on Hydrogenics' technology. In 2019, they signed a cooperation agreement with the Liangjiang New Area of Chongqing to manufacture and develop PEMFC (Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cell) engines and their core components.
